### CPA blunting

![CPA blunting](https://www.radiologymasterclass.co.uk/images/chest-images/cxr_abfgs.jpg?mtime=20210304211313&focal=none)

#### Normally CPA blunting represents 'Effusion'.
#### However, 'Effusion' might be the result of 'Edema'.
#### So, distinguishing Edema from Effusion might be more important than distinguishing Effusion from Edema.
